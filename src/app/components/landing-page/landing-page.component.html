Ez Med is the FIRST and the ONLY platform in Sri Lanka that allows you to book an appointment with your doctor online and get your consultation done via video / audio call.

1. Search for a Doctor

2. Schedule the appointment

3. Wait for the doctor's call

Once your booking is up, doctor (/professional) will contact you. After several attempts to contact you, your booking will be automatically cancelled and you will be refunded (cancellation charges apply*).
